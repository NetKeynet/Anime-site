My Story with Anime

Hi There, I'm Keyrane.

In the previous series of my life, I didn’t used to be an anime girl. HUGE statement compared to how Anime is such a big part of my life now!

Coming to Canada was one BIG experience and school wasn’t so great in Quebec. Before I knew it, I had lost my smile and hype energy.

THAT WHEN IT HAPPENED….

I watched my first anime ever! FARY TAIL. It’s worked wonders on my spirit, weaving a tapestry of emotions that casted away shadows of sadness and brought forth the light of excitement.

I realized that watching anime brings me immense satisfaction.The joy, sadness, rage, and excitement I experience when watching anime are unlike any other. Moreover, it has taught me valuable lessons about the world and people. Today, some of my life decisions are inspired by powerful quotes I remember from all the amazing anime I’ve watched.

Characters I love

Tengen Uzui from Demon slayer.
I personally refer to him as flashy guy hahah. He has a flamboyant personality and live for entertainment. I love how confident and charismatic he is and unafraid to show off his skills and abilities.

Violet Evergarden from Violet Evergarden lol. is such a lovely character. Since a young age, she was a mass-murdering soldier and later became one of the most famous Dolls In the amanuensis’s industry. Despite her expressionless and aloof personality, she is considerate and empathetic and always tries to help her clients no matter what. What I love most about her is her journey from being an “emotionless” tool to becoming more of an expressive person as she learns the meaning of the word “love”.

Sasha, aka Potato Girl from Attack on Titan, is a favorite character with a friendly demeanor and an insatiable appetite. She strikes a balance between being mean and kind, caring yet seemingly heartless when it comes to food. In times of terror, she brings normalcy with humor and a love for food, standing courageously in the face of danger for the sake of her friends and, of course, food.
